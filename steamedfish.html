Steamed Fish Recipe

Description

Chinese teamed fish is a super fast, yet super delicious dish to make. Originated from China, it's a dish that has a soft and juicy fish, paired with a slightly sweet and "ginger-y" sauce. You and the family will for sure love this dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pc of white fish
  • 2 green onions
  • 1/4 julienned ginger
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 1/4 ginger (peeled)
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil

Steps

  1. Prepare your fish by pouring a few drops of sake over it for 5-10 minutes to remove the fishy taste.
  2. Prepare your steamer and make a bed with a couple slices of green onions and ginger on a plate, and then place the fish over it. Place in the steamer and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. In a small pan, add the sake, soy sauce, water, and sugar and cook on medium heat until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Once the fish finishes cooking, place on a clean plate and pour sauce over it.

  6. Pour ginger oil over the fish and sprinkle green onions as garnish.
  7. Serve and enjoy!!
