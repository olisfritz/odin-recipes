Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is probably one of the most famous dishes in this world. There are many variations to this recipe, but today we will stick to the most basic tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 1 medium sized onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • salt (to taste)
  • pepper (to taste)
  • basil
  • pasta

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, saut√© together onions. Once onions are translucent and fragrant add in the garlic. Then add in a pinch of salt.
  2. Add in the tomatoes and simmer down in low heat for 45 minutes.
  3. Once onions are translucent, turn down the leat slightly and add the beef.
  4. Add in the basil and let it cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. On the side, boil water, sprinkle a pinch of salt and a tbsp of olive oil. Then add in the pasta and let it cook per package instructions.
  6. Once pasta is done, drain the water and serve it in a plate.
  7. Top the pasta with a big scoop of the tomato sauce and add in basil as a garnish.
  8. Serve and enjoy!!
